About


Everything you need to know about your computer in one place!
This utility was designed to replace the need to start up multiple utilities to find information about your system as a whole.
Inspiration and more detailed monitoring: CPUID CPU-Z, CPUID HWMon, OpenHardwareMonitor, DxDiag, Task Manager, NZXT CAM.

Open Source


"Why open source?"
The main reason to open source is that Otil is meant to be shared. A lot of effort was put into making Otil what it is today and solves problems faced by many trying to develop similar solutions. Finally, nobody is perfect. Otil will always have room for improvement and a community, more that just one or two developers, is key to its continued growth.
Fellow developers, fork us on GitHub.
